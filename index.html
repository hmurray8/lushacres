Welcome to Lush Acres

Lush Acres will offer a rural lifestyle opportunity within 5km of the Yeppoon CBD and Main Beach. Allotments will be benched to minimise future earthworks requirements, feature sandstone retaining walls, and offer access to all town services (except sewer). Construction is estimated to be completed and titles issued by early 2026 allowing an opportunity to secure your builder, design your dream home, and/or weekend retreat prior to settlement.
